Safety Rules

Please observe the following regulations for your safety and the safety of others.


1. Face Protection

Always wear approved face masks or goggles during play.

2. Firearm Direction

Guns must be pointed in a safe direction when not in use. Never aim at others outside of play.

3. Proper Handling

Treat all rental guns as if they are loaded. Dry-firing is strictly prohibited.

4. Barrel Covers

Use barrel covers whenever guns are not in active play.

5. Eye Protection

Protective eyewear must be worn at all times to prevent injury.

6. Safety Gear

Helmets, vests, and gloves are required during all games.

General Gameplay Rules

Please follow these rules to ensure fair and enjoyable gameplay for everyone.


1. Game Objective

Specific objectives will be explained at the start of each game (e.g., capture the flag, elimination).

2. Game Start/End

The game begins and ends when the referee signals, typically with a whistle.

3. Hits and Eliminations

If you are hit by a BB, you are eliminated and must leave the field or move to the re-spawn zone.

4. No Friendly Fire

Do not shoot teammates or anyone located in the safe zone.

5. Stay Within Boundaries

Remain inside the designated play area. Leaving the field will result in elimination.

Code of Conduct

Please adhere to the following guidelines to maintain a respectful and fair environment.


1. Respect for Others

Treat all players, staff, and referees with respect. Bullying or harassment is not tolerated.

2. No Alcohol or Drugs

Alcohol and drugs are strictly prohibited on the premises at all times.

3. Fair Play

Always follow the rules. Cheating and unsportsmanlike conduct are not allowed.

4. Teamwork

Work together, communicate clearly, and support your teammates during gameplay.

Venue Rules

Respect the venue and follow these rules to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all.


1. Field Boundaries

Stay within the designated playing area at all times. Crossing into restricted zones may result in disqualification.

2. Safe Zones

Rest only in designated safe zones, clearly marked for your safety and comfort.

3. No Unauthorized Areas

Do not enter staff-only or equipment storage areas under any circumstances.

4. Noise Levels

Maintain respectful noise levels. Disruptive sounds or loud music are not permitted during games.

Penalties for Violations

Violations of the rules will result in penalties to ensure safety and fairness for all players.


1. Minor Violations

A warning will be issued for minor infractions (e.g., not wearing safety gear properly).

2. Major Violations

Players engaging in dangerous behavior, cheating, or disrespecting others will be removed from the game. Repeated offenses may result in a permanent ban.

3. Damage to Equipment

If rental equipment is damaged due to negligence or misconduct, the player will be charged for repairs or replacement costs.

Special Considerations

Please keep the following considerations in mind to ensure safe and fair gameplay for all participants.


1. Weather Conditions

Games may be suspended or altered in case of extreme weather (e.g., heavy rain, thunderstorms). Players will be notified in advance.

2. Health Concerns

Players with specific medical conditions (e.g., heart problems, asthma) should inform staff before gameplay begins.

3. Parental Consent

Minors (under 18) must provide written consent from a parent or guardian before being allowed to participate.

Emergency Procedures

In the event of an emergency, please follow these procedures for your safety and the safety of others.


1. First Aid

First-aid kits are available, and staff members are trained in basic first aid assistance.

2. Evacuation

Follow staff instructions in case of an emergency and proceed calmly to the nearest exit point.

3. Emergency Contact

If necessary, staff will assist with contacting emergency services immediately.
