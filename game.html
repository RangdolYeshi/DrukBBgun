Types of Gun Game



Team Death Match

Two teams face off with a limited number of respawn tags per player. The objective is simple: eliminate the opposing team before running out of lives. Fast-paced and highly competitive, this mode is ideal for small groups and quick matches.

  • Teams: 2
  • Respawns: Limited
  • Best For: Small player groups
  • Objective: Eliminate all opponents


Bomb Diffuse Game

A high-intensity scenario where one team is tasked with locating and diffusing a mock bomb while retrieving the warhead. The opposing team defends the warhead at all costs. Unlimited respawns ensure continuous action and large-scale teamwork.

  • Teams: 2
  • Respawns: Unlimited
  • Objective:
  • Attackers: Diffuse the bomb and retrieve the warhead
  • Defenders: Prevent the diffusion and defend the warhead


Fort Wars

Both teams attempt to secure victory by planting their flag on a hill near the opponent's base while also retrieving their own flag from enemy territory. This mode emphasizes strategic movement, defense, and team coordination. Perfect for any group size.

  • Teams: 2
  • Respawns: Flexible (based on match setup)
  • Best For: All player sizes

Step into the ultimate training environment with 24-hour access. This practice arena is your key to mastering the game. Sharpen your reflexes, drill your aim to perfection, and learn the maps inside and out without the pressure of a live match. Stay as long as you want and leave a better player.
